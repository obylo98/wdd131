AOTech Site Plan

Site Name

The name of the site is AOTech. This name was selected because it accurately represents the content and purpose of the website.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a comprehensive overview of my personal portfolio, including my background, skills, projects, and ways to get in touch with me.

Scenarios

  • What are the major projects I have worked on?
  • How can I get in touch with this person?

Color Schema

The primary colors used for this site are:

  • Primary Color: #007BFF (used for headings and accents)
  • Secondary Color: #E9ECEF (used for background and paragraphs)
  • Text Color: #343A40 (used for body text)

Typography

The fonts used for this site are:

  • Font 1: Arial, used for body text
  • Font 2: Georgia, used for headings
